== Installation ==

**From your WordPress dashboard**

* Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
* Search for 'Stickybar Tool'
* Activate 'Stickybar Tool' from your Plugins page.

**From WordPress.org**

* Download 'Stickybar Tool'.
* Upload the 'Stickybar Tool' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
* Activate 'Stickybar Tool' from your Plugins page.
